About us

As the old saying goes "Necessity is the mother of all inventions". BookTime Bookshop was born when our founder Mr Dillon noticed that people in need of books had to commute daily to town just to get their literature needs.

Mr Dillon took his love of books and experience in the hospitality industry to create a unique environment that fascilitates for Bookshop dates as well as a space to unwind.
